How to Play

Controls

Aim with your cursor, left mouse click to fire a bullet

Use 'W' and 'S' to move your tank forward and backwards

Use 'A' and 'D' to rotate your tank left and right

Enemies

Red Enemies

Slow moving slow firing basic enemies

Orange Enemies

these slow moving multi-turret enemies are sure to give you trouble!

Yellow Enemies

these fast shooting fast moving enemies are sure to chase you down!

Blue Enemies

these dangerous turrets have indistructable bullets be sure to practice your evasive manuvers!

Cyan Enemies

these invunerable tanks can shoot and phase through walls, its sure to give you a scare

Purple Turrets

these turrets have heat seaking bullets that chase you around the map!

Mini Tanks
